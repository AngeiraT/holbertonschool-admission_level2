My Story

( Based on a real story!)

Gym people photo created by prostooleh - www.freepik.com

Early this year, I decided to learn to code and it changed my perspective on learning. I am a wife, mom of a 8 year old ,and stepmom of a 15 year old girl. I also worked at three jobs ,as a tutor, swimmer instructor and a technology teacher.

How it all began

During my teaching days I had to learn all the basics about computers and coding. While trying to learn so I can teach my students ,who only knew the basics, I had to commit myself so they could understand and have fun at the same time. It is a challenge. This process grew an interst and a spark into the world of coding.

The Decision

I decided to switch careers to programming which is a completely different field. Now, for me, my tutorial center was online videos, YouTube videos, etc. At first, I didn’t know how to start and I felt somewhat embarrassed and overwhelmed, I mean how will I go about this? Should I just download videos and keep watching? Do I need to look for a book and write everything said in the video? How will I remember everything I’ve been taught? And how will I know that I am doing it correctly? It occurred to me that learning is not that easy, and that’s where my first lesson in learning programming came from.

Stay out of your confort zone

The moment I started filling the application and felt that it was difficult but at the same time I knew if I didn't try I wasn't going to come out to the comfort and found out this could be something I could do for the rest of my life and teach others that everything is possible. Those dreams will come true. I convince myself I am a Dream getter.
